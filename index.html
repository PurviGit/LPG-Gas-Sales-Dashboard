Business Insights Found
📈
West Zone Top Performer
West zone leads all regions at ₹27M revenue. Consistently outperforms next best zone (North at ₹26M) across all quarters.
❄️
Winter Demand Spike
Domestic segment shows 20–25% volume increase during Nov–Feb winter months. Inventory pre-positioning recommended by October.
⚠️
SLA Risk — Central Zone
Central zone SLA drops to ~80%, below the 85% threshold. 2–3 dealers consistently underperform. Route optimization needed.
🏭
Industrial SKU — Highest Unit Revenue
47.5 kg industrial cylinders generate highest revenue per unit despite lower volume. Prioritising this segment improves margins.
🏙️
City-Level Concentration
Jaipur (₹8.4M), Indore (₹7.6M) and Delhi (₹7.5M) are the top 3 cities by revenue — combined they make up 15% of total sales.
📊
Revenue Growth Signal
Revenue grew 12% YoY across all zones. Northeast shows fastest growth rate despite smallest base — expansion opportunity.
How I Built This — Step by Step
1
Data Creation & Structure
Generated 3,287 realistic transaction records across 4 related tables — Sales Transactions, Dealer Master, Monthly Targets and Product Master — using real LPG business logic.
2
Power Query — Data Cleaning
Loaded all 4 Excel sheets into Power Query. Changed column data types, removed nulls, renamed columns to remove underscores and standardised date format to YYYY-MM-DD.
3
Data Modelling — Table Relationships
Created relationships between tables in Model View: Sales → Dealers on Dealer_ID, Sales → Products on Product_SKU, Sales → Targets on Zone and Month fields.
4
DAX Measures — 6 Calculations
Wrote 6 custom DAX measures including Total Revenue, Total Cylinders, Gross Profit, Gross Margin %, SLA % and Revenue vs Target using SUM, DIVIDE, COUNTROWS and FILTER functions.
5
Dashboard Design — 9 Visuals
Built 9 visuals: 5 KPI cards, line chart with 3 customer type lines, donut chart for product mix, horizontal bar chart for zones with individual colors, dealer table, gauge chart and city map.
6
Theme & Formatting
Applied custom dark navy JSON theme with hex colors #070C18 for background, #2563EB for primary accent and unique color per zone. Added Business Insights text box with key findings.
